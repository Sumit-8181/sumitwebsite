Crystal Clear: A Comprehensive Guide to Glass Product Care

Glass is a versatile and elegant material commonly used in furniture and decor. Its transparent and reflective qualities add a touch of sophistication. to any space. Proper care ensures that your glass products remain in pristine condition for years to come. Material Care 1. Avoid Impact: Due to its fragile nature, glass is susceptible to breakage upon impact. Take care to prevent heavy objects or sharp items from coming into contact with glass surfaces. Employ the use of felt pads or coasters to prevent scratches and provide added protection to your glass dining tables and coffee tables. 2. Avoid Abrasive Cleaners: When cleaning glass surfaces, refrain from using abrasive cleaners or rough materials that may scratch the glass. Opt for mild glass cleaners or a mixture of warm water and gentle dish soap. Gently wipe the surface with a soft, lint-free cloth or a microfiber cloth to keep your center tables from scratching. 3. Dry Thoroughly: After cleaning, ensure the glass is thoroughly dried to prevent unsightly water spots or streaks. Use a clean, dry cloth or paper towel to eliminate any moisture from your coffee tables.
